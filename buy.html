Description

"Saranda View" Residence is located in the city center.
It has all the city view, extending to the island of Corfu.
This Residence is one of the best view in Saranda.
This building is in the upper part of the city of Saranda, near the mountain.
In this place you combine a peaceful place without noise, with a fresh mountain air.
Despite the distance from the sea, the building is only 5 minutes away from the central
waterfront and the center of Saranda.
In a small distance from your house, you have a great coffee called “Paradiso”, where you
can sit from early the morning till the latest hour of your day. Also, you have a small market,
to make your daily shopping.
If you decide to go out from city, the nearest attraction you can go is the “Castle of Lekursi”,
it is only 15 min with your car away.
They are offered also indoor parking places from 12.000 E ,according the space pf the
garage.
